**-- WEATHER RELATED --**
**!commands** - shows list of commands (this)
**!weather** - shows a description of the current weather of Dublin
**!tmr** - shows the weather for tomorrow in Dublin
**!hourly** - shows the weather for the next 15 hours in Dublin
**!news** - shows weather related articles in Ireland
**!fact** - shows a fun weather fact
**!gif** - shows a weather gif
**!jacket** - says if you should wear a jacket or not
**-- OTHER --**
**!wordle** - Shows the wordle answer for today
**!ip (IP)** - Shows info on entered IP
**!crypto (coin) (compare)** - shows a real-time chart of the two entered coins on a daily time frame (eg: !crypto BTC ETH)
**!covid (country)** - shows information about covid for the entered country
**!advice** - sends a piece of advice
